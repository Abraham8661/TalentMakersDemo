Who We Are

TalentMakers Foundation empowers underprivileged African youth with world-class IT education and sustainable opportunities, unlocking their potential to drive innovation and economic transformation.

Through strategic partnerships, cutting-edge training, and a commitment to holistic development, we are building a network of skilled professionals who uplift themselves and their communities, fostering collaboration and inspiring lasting change.

Learn more

Transforming Lives Through IT Education

Empower the next generation of tech leaders in Africa through cutting-edge training in fields like Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and Web Development. Our programs are designed to provide underprivileged youth with the skills, mentorship, and opportunities they need to succeed in today’s competitive global tech industry.

Comprehensive Curriculum

Covering emerging technologies such as AI, Data Science, and Web Development.

Industry Partnerships

Collaborating with leading tech companies for mentorship and real-world project experience.

Financial Support

Providing stipends, resources, and tools to ensure full participation without financial barriers.

Job Placement Opportunities

Connecting graduates with internships, jobs, and entrepreneurship resources.

Holistic Development

Focusing on both technical skills and soft skills like leadership and teamwork.
