Youth Empowerment Hackathon 2025

Collaborate, Innovate, Empower: Be Part of the Change!

February 20 - 22, 2025

8AM - 11AM

Lagos, Nigeria

Event Overview

Join us for the Youth Empowerment Hackathon 2025, where underprivileged youth, tech enthusiasts, and industry leaders come together to solve pressing challenges in education, healthcare, and sustainability through technology. This three-day event focuses on fostering innovation, collaboration, and skill development, empowering participants to drive real-world change.

Key Features of the Event

Collaborative Problem-Solving: Work in teams to develop tech solutions addressing societal issues.

Expert Mentorship: Receive guidance from industry professionals and educators.

Workshops & Training: Participate in hands-on sessions on emerging technologies like AI, IoT, and blockchain.

Expert Mentorship: Receive guidance from industry professionals and educators.

Prizes: Exciting rewards for the best innovative solutions.

Prizes and Recognition

1st Prize: $5,000 + Internship Opportunities

2nd Prize: $3,000 + Mentorship Program

3rd Prize: $2,000 + Tech Gadgets

Certificates for all participants.

Schedule
Day 1: Ideation & Team Formation

- Welcome & Keynote Speech

- Presentation of Problem Statements

- Team Building and Brainstorming

Day 2: Development & Workshops

- Coding and Solution Development

- Expert-Led Workshops (e.g., Rapid Prototyping Techniques)

- Mid-Day Progress Review

Day 3: Pitch Day & Awards

- Final Touch-Ups on Projects

- Pitch Presentations to the Jury Panel

- Award Ceremony

Who Should Attend?

Youth aged 18–35 interested in technology and innovation.

Aspiring developers, designers, and entrepreneurs looking to make an impact.

Industry professionals and mentors passionate about empowering youth.

Registration Details

Fee: Free for underprivileged youth (with proof of eligibility).

Deadline to Register: February 5, 2025.

Requirements: A laptop and stable internet connection for virtual participants.
