Frequently Asked Questions

What is TalentMakers Foundation's mission?

TalentMakers Foundation is committed to empowering underprivileged African youth with world-class IT education and creating pathways to sustainable employment, fostering innovation, and driving economic transformation.

Who can join your programs?

Our programs are open to African youth from underprivileged backgrounds who demonstrate a passion for learning and a commitment to personal and professional growth.

Where are your programs offered?

Our programs are conducted both online and in-person, reaching participants across Africa. Check our Programs page for specific locations and availability.

How can I support TalentMakers Foundation?

Who can support us by donating, volunteering, partnering with us, or simply spreading the word about our mission and programs..

How can I stay updated on events and initiatives?

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, or regularly check our website for updates on programs, events, and impact stories.

How can I volunteer with TalentMakers Foundation?

Fill out the volunteer application form on our "Get Involved" page, or contact us directly for more details.
