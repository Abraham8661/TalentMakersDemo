Dave Journey with TalentMakers

John Paul
Content manager, TalentMakers

March 8, 2025

5 mins read

Dave Okello’s story is one of resilience, ambition, and transformation. Growing up in a rural Kenyan village, Dave faced significant challenges—limited access to quality education, financial hardships, and a lack of resources to pursue his dreams. Yet, he possessed an undeniable passion for technology, spending countless hours tinkering with outdated computers at a local community center. Little did he know, his life would take a dramatic turn when he discovered TalentMakers Foundation.

The Beginning of the Journey In 2023, Dave came across a flyer for TalentMakers Foundation’s Web Development Bootcamp while attending a community workshop. Intrigued by the opportunity, he applied despite doubts about his qualifications. To his surprise and delight, Dave was selected for the program. “It felt like a dream come true,” he recalls. “I finally had a chance to learn the skills I’d always dreamed of.” The bootcamp provided Dave with more than just technical knowledge. Over the course of six months, he mastered HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, building dynamic websites and gaining a strong foundation in coding. TalentMakers also offered holistic support, including a monthly stipend, mentorship from industry professionals, and career readiness training. “The mentors were incredible,” Dave shares. “They didn’t just teach me how to code; they taught me how to think like a problem-solver.”

Overcoming Challenges While the program was rigorous, Dave’s determination kept him going. Balancing studies with his responsibilities at home was not easy, but he received unwavering support from his peers and the TalentMakers team. One of his proudest moments was completing a group project where he and his team developed a web-based application to help small farmers track crop yields and manage inventory. “It wasn’t just about writing code; it was about creating something meaningful,” Dave explains. “That project showed me the power of technology to solve real-world problems.”

A New Beginning Upon graduating, Dave was offered an internship with a leading tech firm in Nairobi, facilitated by TalentMakers’ job placement program. Within a year, he transitioned into a full-time role as a front-end developer, contributing to innovative projects and earning a stable income that transformed his family’s financial situation.

Dave didn’t stop there. Inspired by the impact of TalentMakers, he began volunteering as a mentor for new cohorts, sharing his experiences and encouraging others to dream big. “Helping others grow reminds me of where I started,” he says. “It’s my way of giving back.”

Looking Ahead Today, Dave is a shining example of TalentMakers Foundation’s mission in action. His journey from a curious youth in a small village to a successful developer and mentor demonstrates the transformative power of education and opportunity. “TalentMakers didn’t just teach me skills; they gave me a platform to believe in myself and make a difference,” Dave reflects. As he looks to the future, Dave aspires to launch his own tech startup, focusing on solutions that empower rural communities like the one he grew up in. His story continues to inspire others, proving that with the right support, no dream is too big to achieve.
