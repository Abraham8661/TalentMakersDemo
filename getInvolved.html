Get involved

Join Us in Empowering Lives Through Technology

Your involvement can make a lasting impact. Whether you volunteer, partner, or donate, you’re helping us create a brighter future for Africa’s youth.

Ways to Get Involved

1

Volunteer Your Time

Become a mentor, trainer, or workshop facilitator and share your expertise with our talented youth. Your guidance can inspire and empower the next generation of tech leaders.

Opportunities Include:

Mentoring participants in coding, design, and soft skills.

Conducting workshops on emerging technologies.

Supporting community events like hackathons and job fairs.

2

Partner with Us

Collaborate with us to provide resources, curriculum support, and job opportunities for our graduates. Together, we can drive innovation and create sustainable employment pathways.

Ways to Partner:

Offer internships and job placements for program graduates.

Sponsor a training cohort or specific program..

Share expertise through joint projects and collaborations.

3

Donate to Support Our Mission

Your financial contributions directly impact our ability to provide cutting-edge education, resources, and opportunities for youth in need. Every donation fuels change and drives transformation.

What Your Donation Supports:

Scholarships for participants.

Resources like laptops and internet access.

The chance to give back by mentoring others.
